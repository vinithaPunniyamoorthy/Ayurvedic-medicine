Ayurvedic Herbs

Common Ayurvedic Herbs

Turmeric

Known for its anti-inflammatory properties. Contains curcumin, which has powerful antioxidant effects. Used for joint pain, digestive issues, and skin conditions.

Uses: Arthritis, indigestion, wound healing

Ashwagandha

An adaptogenic herb that helps the body manage stress. Known as "Indian ginseng" for its rejuvenating properties.

Uses: Stress relief, improved sleep, immune support, cognitive function

Ginger

A warming herb that aids digestion and reduces nausea. Has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Uses: Nausea, motion sickness, indigestion, cold and flu symptoms

Other Popular Herbs

Holy Basil (Tulsi)

Stress relief, immune support, respiratory health.

Triphala

Digestive health, detoxification, eye health.

Brahmi

Memory enhancement, cognitive function, anxiety reduction.

Neem

Skin health, blood purification, dental care.
